Felyx.city is an absurdist website based on the Felyx's company tagline "Beat the Streets" and the impact of shared mobility services on public space. Where shared becomes associated not with taking care of common resources but a careless clutter of vehicles all over the city.

This website was created by Pedro Gil Farias using Polycam to 3D scan the Felyx model and then model-viewer to display it.

If you want a physical embodiment of this website, I've also made a screenprint that you can buy here.
