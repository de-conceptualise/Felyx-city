Felyx.city is an absurdist website based on the Felyx's company tagline "Beat the Streets" and their approach to deploying shared electric mopeds all over the city. Becoming a staple of carelessness in public space.

This website was created by Pedro Gil Farias and is part of a larger exploration of the impact of shared mobility in public space. If you want a physical embodiment of this website, I've also made a screenprint that you can buy here.
